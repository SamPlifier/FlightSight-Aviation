About Our Staff

Mauricio Castro

Owner, CEO, Aircraft & Drone Pilot

Mauricio is originally from El Salvador and arrived in Boston in 1980. After studying electronics science and technology, he worked in the industry for more than 14 years, mainly in research and development. In 1995, he moved to Chapel Hill, North Carolina, where he directed a statewide non-profit that worked to build bridges between immigrant communities and the larger community. Mauricio led many initiatives which, focused on connecting people of multi-faith and multi-racial backgrounds with local and state agencies, and resources to improve relations and conditions.

He started in the aviation industry in 2009 when he enrolled for training of manned aircraft. Today, he is a certified pilot, a commercial unmanned pilot (UAS), and an avid participant and supporter of aviation education through formal trainings with various national and local flight agencies. Flight safety is Mauricio's primary focus, followed by a strong emphasis on understanding federal and state aviation regulations. He believes real pilots are always learning and Mauricio runs his company with that in mind, to the benefit of himself and his clients. He also holds an FCC General class amateur radio license.

Sam Davis-Castro

Web Developer, Graphic Designer & Editor

Sam is the owner's son, and mostly grew up in Chapel Hill. After earning a Bachelor of Music Degree from The University of North Carolina School of the Arts, he spent 5 years teaching one on one music lessons at The Chapel Hill School of Musical Arts on 7 different instruments. Sam taught himself vector based graphic design to deliver custom learning materials to his diverse students ranging from elementary ages through adult.

Later, he began studying web design and development in between responsibilities as a bilingual customer service agent and supervisor. Eventually, he organized and shared his suite of tools on an internal website which, included searchable online maps in 5 cities with over 700 points, charts, social media feeds, promotions and succinct memo's collected from local management in 9 cities. He left to attend The Iron Yard in September of 2016 and since graduation in December, he continues to study topics including HTML5, CSS3, Sass, JavaScript, jQuery, responsive design, Bootstrap, Angular, Grunt, AJAX, RESTful API's, JSON, React, PHP, Python and SQLite. He is currently a Junior Optimization Engineer in Raleigh and is the developer of this website.
